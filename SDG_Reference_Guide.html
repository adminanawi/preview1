How Eswatini’s Initiatives Map to Global Goals

The Kingdom of Eswatini’s Government Programme of Action (2024/25–2028/29) aligns with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). Of the 289 tracked initiatives, 140 are explicitly linked to one or more SDGs. Below is a reference guide showing each SDG, its relevance to Eswatini’s government programmes, and the number of initiatives that support it.
