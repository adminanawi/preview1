This hub consolidates performance data from all 21 parliamentary annual reports into three purpose-built dashboards aligned to the Government Programme of Action (PoA) 2024/2025–2028/2029. Each dashboard addresses a distinct question: What is government doing? — How well is government performing? — How effectively is government spending?

Government Initiatives Tracker

Answers the question: What is government doing?


A catalogue of all active government initiatives, programmes, and projects drawn from all 21 ministerial reports. Each initiative is mapped to its PoA objective, implementation status, and source ministry, enabling the Prime Minister to track progress across the full scope of the government agenda in a single view.


Use this dashboard to identify which objectives have the most activity, which initiatives are on track versus delayed, and to drill down into individual ministry programme portfolios.

Dashboard 2
Operational Performance

Answers the question: How well is government performing?


Tracks quantified operational and service delivery indicators drawn from the parliamentary reports — targets versus actuals — organised by the six PoA objectives. Each objective page presents KPI cards, visualisations, and expandable ministry panels with indicator-level status pills: Exceeded, On Track, Below Target, or Gap.


Use this dashboard for PM briefings, Cabinet performance reviews, and to identify where service delivery is meeting — or falling short of — the PoA commitments for the current financial year.

Dashboard 3
Budget Absorption Cross-Cut

Answers the question: How effectively is government spending?


A cross-ministry fiscal analysis comparing each ministry's annual budget allocation against actual and committed expenditure for FY 2024/25. Budget absorption rates are tracked for all 19 ministries with available data, grouped by PoA objective, and rated: Strong (≥90%), On Track (80–89%), Below Target (60–79%), or Concern (<60%).


Use this dashboard to identify ministries with underspending risk or over-expenditure pressure, assess the fiscal distribution across the six PoA objectives, and support Supplementary Budget and National Budget cycle decisions.

How to Use This Suite

1

For Cabinet or Parliamentary Briefings

Open the Operational Performance Dashboard and navigate to the relevant PoA objective tab. Use the ministry panels to discuss specific indicator performance against targets. Status pills provide an at-a-glance readiness picture.

2

For Programme Oversight

Open the Initiatives Tracker and filter by ministry or PoA objective. Review implementation status across all active programmes. Use the status and source filters to surface delayed or off-track initiatives requiring ministerial attention.

3

For Budget & Fiscal Review

Open the Budget Absorption Dashboard to review expenditure performance by ministry and objective. Identify under-absorbing ministries early, flag over-expenditure pressures, and assess which objectives are receiving proportionate resourcing.
